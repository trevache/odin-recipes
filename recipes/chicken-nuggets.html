Chicken Nuggets Recipe

Description

Chicken nuggets are a real kid-pleaser! Adults will enjoy this recipe for a tasty appetizer, too! Serve warm, with your favorite dipping sauces if desired.

Ingredients

  • Chicken: Three skinless, boneless chicken breasts will make about six servings.
  • Bread crumbs: Use store-bought or homemade Italian seasoned bread crumbs.
  • Cheese: You'll need Â½ cup of Parmesan cheese.
  • Seasonings: Season the chicken nuggets with dried basil, dried thyme, and salt.
  • Butter: Dip the nuggets in melted butter before coating them in bread crumbs.

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken breasts into nuggets.
  2. Mix the bread crumbs, cheese, and seasonings.
  3. Dip the nuggets in melted butter, then coat them in the bread crumb mixture.
  4. Bake until the chicken is brown and cooked through.
